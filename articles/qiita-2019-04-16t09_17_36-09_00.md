### 1. Add pry-rails to Gemfile 

     + gem 'pry-rails' 

### 2. Install the Gem 

`$ bundle install`

 
### 3. Launch the Rails Console 

`$ rails console`

 
* * *

Point: Put pry-rails instead of pry.
