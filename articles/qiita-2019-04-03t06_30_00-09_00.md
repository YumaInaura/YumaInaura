とても簡単。

```json
$ echo "[1,2,3]" | jq 'reverse'
[
  3,
  2,
  1
]
```

```json
$ echo "[[1,2],[3,4],[5,6]]" | jq '.[] | reverse'
[
  2,
  1
]
[
  4,
  3
]
[
  6,
  5
]
```
