```ruby
[27] pry(main)> csv = CSV.new(StringIO.new("A,B,C\nX,Y,Z"))
=> <#CSV io_type:StringIO encoding:UTF-8 lineno:0 col_sep:"," row_sep:"\n" quote_char:"\"">
[28] pry(main)> csv.readlines
=> [["A", "B", "C"], ["X", "Y", "Z"]]

```
